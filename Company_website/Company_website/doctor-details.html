Dr. Sarah Johnson

Ph.D. in Physical Therapy, Certified Orthopedic Specialist

Dr. Sarah Johnson is the founder and lead physiotherapist at PHYSIO EXERCISES. With over 15 years of experience in the field, she has established herself as a leading expert in orthopedic rehabilitation and sports medicine.

After completing her undergraduate studies at the University of California, Dr. Johnson pursued her Ph.D. in Physical Therapy at Johns Hopkins University, where she graduated with honors. Her doctoral research focused on innovative approaches to treating chronic back pain, which has since been published in several prestigious medical journals.

Prior to founding PHYSIO EXERCISES in 2010, Dr. Johnson worked at several renowned hospitals and sports rehabilitation centers, including the Sports Medicine Clinic at Stanford University and the Rehabilitation Center at Mount Sinai Hospital. These experiences allowed her to work with diverse patient populations, from professional athletes recovering from injuries to elderly patients managing age-related mobility issues.

Dr. Johnson's approach to physiotherapy combines evidence-based treatments with personalized care. She believes in treating not just the symptoms but addressing the root causes of physical discomfort. Her holistic methodology incorporates manual therapy, therapeutic exercises, and patient education to ensure comprehensive recovery and long-term well-being.
