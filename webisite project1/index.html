HAVE QUESTIONS?

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

Our age groups are based on common physical therapy needs at different life stages. If you're between categories, we recommend starting with the program for your chronological age. During your initial assessment, we'll fine-tune the program to match your specific physical condition and goals.
We offer a selection of free basic exercises for each age group. However, our personalized programs, advanced routines, and progress tracking features require a subscription. You can try our premium features with a 7-day free trial before committing to a subscription.
Our intake form includes detailed questions about your medical history. Based on your information, our system will automatically modify exercises to accommodate your condition. For complex cases, we recommend starting with an in-person consultation where our physiotherapists can create a fully customized program.
Most programs recommend 3-5 sessions per week, each lasting 20-45 minutes depending on your specific program. Consistency is more important than duration - we'd rather see you do 15 minutes daily than one long session weekly. Our app will create a personalized schedule based on your goals and availability.
Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your subscription at any time. When upgrading, you'll pay the prorated difference for the remainder of your billing cycle. Downgrades take effect at your next billing date. All changes can be made easily through your account dashboard.
