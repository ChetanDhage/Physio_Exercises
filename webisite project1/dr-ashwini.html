About Dr. Ashwini

Hi, I am Dr.Ashwini. I am a dedicated physiotherapist specialising in musculoskeletal conditions. With a Bachelor's and Master's degree in Physiotherapy, I focus on providing personalised, evidence-based care to address chronic pain, joint discomfort, and work-related strain.

My approach aims to identify the root cause of pain, improve mobility, and prevent future injuries, empowering patients to regain their quality of life and live actively. I believe in treating not just the symptoms but the whole person, creating comprehensive treatment plans that address both immediate concerns and long-term health goals.

Throughout my career, I've worked with patients of all ages and backgrounds, from athletes recovering from sports injuries to office workers dealing with repetitive strain issues. This diverse experience has given me a unique perspective on how to tailor treatments to individual needs and lifestyles.

I'm committed to continuing education and staying current with the latest research and techniques in physiotherapy. This allows me to offer my patients the most effective, evidence-based treatments available.
